Meta and UTF-8

This webpage is a portfolio development assessment assigned by Leeds Beckett University, for website development students of Level 4 - Semester 2. This webpage includes metadata tags and UTF-8 example.

Let's see the Nepali Unicode translation of some basic english phrases:

  • Hello: नमस्ते (Namaste)
  • Good Morning: शुभ - प्रभात (shubha-prabhat)
  • What's your name ?: तिम्रो नाम के हो ? (Timro naam ke ho ?)
  • Nice to meet you: तिमीलाई भेटेर खुशी लाग्यो (Timilai bhetera khusi lagyo)
  • See you soon: छिट्टै भेटौँला (Chhetai bhataula)
